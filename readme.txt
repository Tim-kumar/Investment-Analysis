Business Objective:

The objective is to identify the best sectors, countries, and a suitable investment type(venture, seed, angel, grant etc) for making investments

Constraints:

1. Investment amount is between 5 to 15 million USD per round of investment
2. Investment only in English-speaking countries


Inputs:

companies.txt -> information about companies
rounds2.csv   -> funding raised by companies
mapping.csv   -> information about sectors
